A Noita death visualizer

Usage

Click the button below and open your Noita sessions folder. Your session files are then processed, after which the map should be displayed in the gray box below. After the image is generated, you can save it by right-clicking it and choosing Save Image As.

On Windows, the sessions folder is located at:

C:\Users\%USERNAME%\AppData\LocalLow\Nolla_Games_Noita\save00\stats\sessions
